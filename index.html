Welcome to the KR Network

KR is a grassroots community platform built by creatives, for creatives.

🌐 Social platforms today are no longer built for creatives to grow & be seen, KR is a community platform & network where we artists can found for what we do, rather than who we are.

How KR works:

  • Be Discovered: Make an account & connect with others to get seen on our network map & discover feed.
  • Collaborate: Check the collaboration page to see what upcoming opportunities have been posted by others on the network.
  • Platform Your Community: Connect with your friends to share your audience with them.

The website is live, and always growing. One thing will never change: it will always belong to the creatives.
